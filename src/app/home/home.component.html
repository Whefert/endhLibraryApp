Bob Clarey

Mr. Clarey was an Attorney and much more. He was in Navy JAG, worked in the Eastern District U.S. Attorney's Office, was a bureau chief at the Nassau County District Attorney's office, and had a private practice. He was a criminal lawyer and a trial lawyer. He helped many young men get on the right track.

Barry Ricketts
